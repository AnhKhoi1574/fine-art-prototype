3D ARTIFACT

Decoration Pot

Decoration pot from mainland China. However, Vietnam officials held the object since Vietnamese people controlled the land when it was produced. This pot was decorated with scenes from Vietnamese mythology.

Make-shift Mortar

Fully crafted rectangular Mortar and Grinding Stone. The shallow mortar likely ground seeds and spices. This tool was constructed from northern Vietnam stones. This was shaped by ancient humans.

Vintage pepper grinder

This grinder was used by many 1200s and 1300s homes. However, wealthy people and government officials used this as a luxury item throughout the 1000s to 1100s.

Royal Teapot

Custom china-and-ceramic teapot for an anonymous government official. Its outstanding condition shows the teapot was kept as a collection and decoration.
